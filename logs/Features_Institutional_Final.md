# Dawn: Institutional Platform - Final Feature Set
**Academic Focus: pure Institutional Learning Management System (LMS)**

---

### 1. Academic & Batch Management
- **Batch Hierarchy**: Organized enrollment based on institutional batches (e.g., Spring 2026).
- **Module Enrollment**: Students are enrolled in academic modules (courses) assigned to their batch.
- **Batch-Based Dashboards**: Admins can view analytics filtered by student cohorts and batches.

### 2. Result & Transcript Engine
- **Automated Grading**: Grades are automatically calculated from assignment submissions (100% weight).
- **Transcript Generation**: Students can view and download their academic transcripts showing letter grades, grade points, and cumulative GPAs.
- **Admin Transcript Review**: Staff and Admins can query any student's academic standing and transcript history.

### 3. Financial & Tuition System
- **Semester Invoices**: Automated tuition billing per semester/batch.
- **Tuition Tracking**: Students can view their payment history and current outstanding balances for their current academic level.
- **Institutional Revenue Analytics**: Admin view of total revenue collected from completed tuition payments.

### 4. Advanced Learning Analytics
- **Learning Velocity**: session tracking with a 30-second heartbeat for accurate study-time reporting.
- **Progress Synchronization**: Course progress increases by exactly 10% per lesson completed.
- **Engagement Heatmaps**: Daily and weekly activity charts showing student interaction time.

### 5. Instructor Studio
- **Roster Management**: Teachers can view the full student roster for their assigned modules.
- **Assignment Control**: Instructors can manage academic assignments and grade submissions.
- **Instructor Dashboard**: Overview of student audience size and average module progress.

### 6. Platform Administration
- **Faculty Onboarding**: Bulk generation of teacher accounts with mandatory "Force Password Change" on first login for security.
- **Institutional Branding**: Dynamic logo and title configuration for the institution.
- **Direct Support Inquiries**: Students can submit academic and technical support tickets directly to the administration.

### 7. AI Academic Assistant (AITutor)
- **Contextual Knowledge**: AI assistant trained on platform modules to provide 24/7 academic support.
- **Session-Aware Assistance**: The AI tracks tutorial session durations to contribute to the student's learning velocity.
